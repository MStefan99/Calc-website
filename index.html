About Annushka Project

We are Mariia, Mikhail and Rukhsora and we are students from Helsinki Metropolia University of Applied Sciences IT Degree students. We represent Annushka project team that is enthusiastic about programming. Once there was a situation that we could not find free Calculator for our assignments where we needed to turn decimal numbers into binary. Time passed and now we have a chance to make one, to challenge our programming skills and be useful for other students with the same issue. We hope that our first Application - Programmer’s calculator will handle your mathematical operations, there will be provided comfortably designed interface and confidence in the accurateness of your solutions.
